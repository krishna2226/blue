System Requirements

Recommended hardware requirements:

1. Computer

A modern multi-core processor (e.g., Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5) and at least 8GB of RAM are recommended.

2. Display

A high-resolution display (e.g., Full HD or higher) can enhance productivity by providing ample screen real estate for coding, design work, and testing across different devices and resolutions.

3. Internet Connection

A stable internet connection is crucial for accessing online resources, collaborating with team members, and testing website functionality across various devices and browsers.

4. Backup Solution
